FAQs
  • ▶ General overview
    What is a Smart City?
    A smart city uses technology and data-driven solutions to improve citizens’ lives, sustainability, service delivery, and foster economic growth and a better quality of life.
  • ▶ How smart cities work
    Smart cities integrate IoT, data analytics, and digital platforms to manage resources efficiently and deliver better public services.
  • ▶ Sustainability and community
    They promote green initiatives, waste management, and community engagement for inclusive and sustainable growth.
  • ▶ Security and privacy
    Smart cities ensure data privacy and security through robust cybersecurity measures and transparent data policies.
